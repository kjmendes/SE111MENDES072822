My name is Kyesha Mendes. My birthday is July 19th, I am 31 years old. I am from Providence Rhode Island

First i went to holliman Elementary School. Then i went to Aldritch during middle school. I went to Pilgrim High School and graduated in 2019. After that i went to CCRI for General Studies and graduated in 2021. I am currently at NEIT for Cybersecurity/Networking.

I enjoy

I like spending time and playing with my dog. I also enjoy watching anime and playing video games. My current favorite game is Elden Ring. I like working out, Archery, and spending time with my family. Every year I attend Comic Con in Providence. I enjoy meeting some of the celebrities, browsing at the vendor booths, and the photo ops available.
